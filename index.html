The List.

Words of relationship 'wisdom' from an older to a younger woman on her 18th birthday.

Use it wisely.


The bad stuff
  • Avoid men who tell you they love you within a month. They just want to own you.
  • Don't date men who know how to dance. A common misconception. Men who can dance have egos that far outweigh their personalities.
  • Don't date men who tell you to not do things - 'because they care about you'. They are controlling you through guilt.
  • Don't date men who tell you to dress a certain way
  • Never date a man who owns reptiles. I mean...why?
  • Never date a man who is rude to waiters. Don't have friends who are rude to waiters. Those people are assholes.
  • Don't date a bad drunk
  • Don't date a man who does drugs. They're stupid
  • Don't do drugs. They're stupid
  • Don't date men who cry
  • Don't date musicians. They're the worst.
  • Did he buy you a drink? You don't need to sleep with him.
  • Did he take you out? You don't need to sleep with him.
  • Have you slept with him before? You don't need to sleep with him now. You don't owe anyone sex. Ever
  • Think long and hard about long-distance relationships. You will live some strange half life where you never really feel settled.
  • Don't date men in the armed forces, unless you're also in the forces, or you really know what you're doing.
  • Never forgive a man for hitting you. He will do it again.
  • Don't date a man who your friends and parents don't like
  • Don't date a man who actively tries to keep you away from your friends or from doing hobbies
  • Don't date a man who pressures you to do something in bed that you don't want to do
  • Don't date men who 'don't like wearing condoms'. Grow up.
  • Don't date a man with a moustache. Obviously.
  • Never date a man who got a tattoo while drunk
  • Beware of gaslighters. They will ruin your life.
  • Think twice about a man who is taken. If a man is willing to cheat with you then he's willing to cheat on you.
  • DO NOT DATE A MAN WHO SUPPORTS THE LEAVE CAMPAIGN
It get's better...
  • Trust people. If they break that trust it's on them, and it's not your fault.
  • Go for a man who's kind. Kind is not boring. It's essential.
  • Trust your intuition, it's OK, we're women.
  • Do be supportive of their hobbies and likes and expect their support back.
  • Do give people a chance. Love isn't always immediate. And may not be what you imagined.
  • A good sense of humour is a very good sign.
  • Remember, a boyfriend is a friend first.
  • Men cannot multitask. It's a fact. Do be patient.
  • Men are shit at dealing with illness. Do have the patient of a saint.
  • Men aren't so good at laughing at themselves. Go gentle.
  • Hey, we're not perfect either....I hear...
  • Do be honest. Brutally so. Communication is the backbone of all relationships.
  • Being single is better than being with the wrong person. Your relationship with yourself is the most important.
  • If in doubt, always ask your Mum. She knows all.
